Your Questions, Answered

If you have more questions, feel free to contact us.

What is LearnGen.ai?

LearnGen.ai is an AI-powered learning platform that provides personalized learning paths, instant tutoring, and progress tracking to help students achieve their academic goals.

How does the AI Tutor work?

The AI Tutor uses advanced natural language processing to answer your questions, explain concepts, and provide step-by-step guidance in real-time.

Is LearnGen.ai free to use?

LearnGen.ai offers a free plan with basic features. For advanced features like personalized learning paths and smart quizzes, you can upgrade to a paid plan.

What subjects does LearnGen.ai support?

LearnGen.ai supports a wide range of subjects, including mathematics, science, history, and more. It is designed to adapt to your learning needs.

How can I track my progress?

You can track your progress through detailed analytics and reports available in your dashboard. These insights help you identify areas for improvement.

How do I contact support?

You can contact our support team by emailing us at support@learngenie.ai or calling us at +1 234 567 890.
